How Does It Work?

To get started, you simply create a free account at Trello.com, download one of the pre-made "boards" to the right and import it.

Follow the instructions inside of the board, read the "Start Here" Card and become famliar with your new workspace.

This is likely an entirely new process for you, so be patient with yourself.

A great place to start is by completing the "Acronyms & Terminology" exercise in which you slowly (at your own pace) build up a broad knowledge of terms relevant to your next chapter.

Once you feel like you're in a good place to go further, simply click the button below and we'll schedule time to discuss next steps.
